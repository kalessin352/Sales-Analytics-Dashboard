# Sales Analytics Dashboard

Interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing historical sales data across Retail and Warehouse channels (2017–2020).

---

## Business Objective

The goal of this project was to explore historical sales data and create an interactive dashboard that allows users to:

- compare Retail and Warehouse sales,
- identify top-performing suppliers,
- analyze monthly sales trends,
- examine product category distribution,
- filter results by year and supplier.

---

## Key Insights

- Warehouse sales account for approximately **78%** of total sales.
- Retail represents around **22%** of total sales.
- Beer products generate the largest share of sales (~77%).
- CROWN IMPORTS is the highest-performing supplier.
- Sales show recurring seasonal peaks during the analyzed period.
